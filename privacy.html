Important Notes on Functionality

The Application enhances photos using on-device processing and filters. Photos and edits are processed locally on your device unless you explicitly share them. The Application does not change your device's physical camera hardware capabilities.

Information Collection and Use

The Application may collect the following information:

  • Device information (e.g., OS version, device model)
  • App usage analytics (e.g., screens visited, time spent)

Device Permissions

The Application requests permissions only to provide core features:

  • Camera: required to show live preview and capture photos.
  • Microphone: required to record video with sound.
  • Photos/Media: required to import images and save edits to your gallery. On Android 13+ this may include READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / READ_MEDIA_VIDEO. On older versions it may use storage permissions.
  • Notifications (optional): used to schedule local reminders (for example, a notification that your photo is ready).

Advertising

The Application displays ads using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect advertising identifiers, device information, and usage data for ad delivery and measurement. You can reset your advertising ID in your device settings.
